The Problem: Cascading Failures

  • Modern companies run hundreds of interconnected microservices.
  • When ONE service fails, it can crash EVERYTHING (cascading failure).
  • Manual monitoring is slow — by the time engineers notice, the damage is done.
  • SLA violations lead to financial penalties and lost trust.
